About Us

Founded by Engr. Fabulous J. Nweke, our company stands as a symbol of resilience and innovation across three vital sectors: agriculture, oil and gas, and logistics. What began as a modest vision has evolved into a dynamic enterprise dedicated to empowering communities, driving sustainable growth, and building a legacy that stands the test of time.

Our operations span livestock farming, rice processing and production, fuel stations, cooking gas plants, and haulage logistics. Each division plays a crucial role in ensuring food security, energy accessibility, and efficient transportation across regions.

We are driven by a commitment to excellence, ethical leadership, and sustainable practices—ensuring that every aspect of our business contributes positively to people, the planet, and prosperity.

A Legacy of Resilience

Resilience is the foundation of our story. From humble beginnings to a diversified conglomerate, every milestone reflects a deep-rooted belief that strength is built through perseverance, integrity, and innovation. Under the visionary leadership of Engr. Fabulous J. Nweke, we continue to build a brand that not only endures but also inspires.
