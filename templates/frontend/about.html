About Fabstar Limited

Delivering freshness, quality, and trust — from our farms to your table.

Our Story

FabStar Limited began with a simple vision to empower farmers and agribusinesses with reliable access to quality agricultural products, livestocks, and oil and gas. Over the years, we've grown into a trusted partner for agricultural development, offering innovative solutions and personalized support to ensure every harvest is a success.

From smallholder farmers to large commercial farms, we work together to grow a sustainable agricultural future for generations to come.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality agricultural products that help farmers increase productivity, enhance soil health, and cultivate sustainable growth in every region we serve.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading agribusiness brand, transforming lives by connecting innovation with the heart of farming — people, land, and nature.

Our Core Values

Integrity

Honest dealings and transparent practices in everything we do.

Sustainability

Farming practices that care for people and the planet.

Innovation

Modern tools and ideas driving the future of agriculture.

Community

Empowering farmers and creating value through collaboration.
